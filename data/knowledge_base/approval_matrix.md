# Approval Matrix

Purchases below $5,000 require requester manager approval. Purchases from $5,000 to $24,999 require department manager and Procurement approval.

# Standard Review

Purchases from $25,000 to $99,999 require Department VP, Procurement, and Security review when software or sensitive data is involved.

# Enterprise Review

Purchases of $100,000 or more require CFO, Legal, Security, and Procurement Director approval. Contract signature may occur only after all required approvals are recorded.

# Renewal Review

Renewals should be reviewed at least 30 days before the renewal date. Auto-renewal clauses longer than one year require Legal review.
