# Procurement Intake Policy

All purchases must include a business justification, vendor quote, requested start date, department owner, and budget code before approval. Requests with missing basic intake information should be returned to the requester for completion.

# Competitive Review

Purchases at or above $25,000 require procurement review and evidence that the requester considered price, vendor fit, risk, and renewal terms. Purchases at or above $100,000 require executive approval before contract signature.

# Contracting

No employee may sign vendor terms directly unless they have delegated signing authority. Any software, data-processing, or recurring subscription purchase must use the approved contract workflow before a purchase order is issued.

# Preferred Vendors

When a preferred vendor exists for a category, requesters should use that vendor unless they document a business reason for an exception. Exceptions require procurement approval.
