# Responsible AI and Automation

AI agents may assist procurement decisions but must not become the sole approver for financial commitments, vendor risk acceptance, or legal terms. Human owners remain accountable for final approvals.

# Transparency

The agent must show policy evidence and tool results used to produce a recommendation. Users should be able to inspect why a request was routed, escalated, or blocked.

# Bias and Fairness

Vendor recommendations must not unfairly penalize small or new vendors. Risk decisions should be based on documented security, financial, operational, and compliance evidence rather than assumptions about vendor size or geography.

# Privacy

Requests should avoid unnecessary personal information. Case records should store only the details required for audit, approval, and compliance.
